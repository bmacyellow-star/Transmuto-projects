# Moodboard: Nanny Agency CRM

> Created: 2026-03-08
> Status: Approved

---

## Visual Direction

Warm minimal. Modern but approachable — professional without feeling corporate or cold. The product should feel like a well-designed space: calm, organised, trustworthy. Signals care and competence. Nothing flashy, nothing clinical.

## Colour Mood

- **Light only** — no dark mode for MVP
- **Canvas:** Warm neutral — not stark white. Think off-white / warm grey / light beige (#FAFAF8 range)
- **Accent energy:** Neutral with a pop — one confident accent colour against muted warm neutrals
- **Accent direction:** Warm teal or sage green (professional + approachable for childcare context)
- **Supporting colours:** Muted — soft coral for warnings, warm amber for attention, sage for success
- **Avoid:** Cold blue-grey, high-saturation primaries, pure white backgrounds

---

## Typography Mood

- **Headings:** Warmer typeface — Plus Jakarta Sans or DM Sans. Friendly, modern, slightly rounded.
- **Body/UI:** Inter — readability first, proven at all sizes
- **Density:** Balanced — comfortable line heights, not cramped
- **Feel:** Professional warmth. Not geometric/sterile, not playful/casual.

---

## Interaction Mood

- **Speed:** Smooth — 150-200ms transitions, nothing jarring
- **Feedback:** Subtle — soft hover states, gentle colour shifts
- **Loading:** Skeleton screens (not spinners) — maintains layout calm
- **Micro-interactions:** Minimal — subtle scale on button press, smooth accordion/drawer opens
- **Overall:** The UI should feel quiet. Things happen without drama.
